## 4. Data Security

### How We Protect Your Data

**In Transit (data traveling to our server):**
- 🔒 HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2+)
- All communication encrypted
- Cannot be intercepted

**At Rest (data stored on our server):**
- 🔒 Database encryption
- Passwords hashed with Argon2 (not reversible)
- Access controls (only authorized staff)
- Regular backups

**Authentication:**
- 🔒 JWT tokens (secure session keys)
- Token expiration after period of inactivity
- Password complexity requirements:
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - Mix of upper/lowercase
  - Numbers or special characters

### Security Measures

- ✅ Firewalls and intrusion detection
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Encrypted database backups
- ✅ No storage of sensitive card data (numbers, CVV)
- ✅ SQL injection prevention
- ✅ XSS (cross-site scripting) protection

### What We Can't Guarantee

- No security is 100% perfect
- Unauthorized access is always a small risk
- Large-scale breaches can happen to any company
- You are responsible for password security

**If you think your account is compromised:**
1. Change your password immediately
2. Email: security@cardbenefitstacker.com
3. We will investigate and respond within 24 hours

## 10. Cookies & Tracking

- `preferences` — Remembers your settings

**Optional (improve experience):**
- `analytics` — Track usage (can disable)

### How to Manage Cookies

**Disable in browser:**

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
2. Choose: Block all cookies (app won't work) or see options

**Firefox:**
1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
2. Choose: Reject all (app won't work) or see options

**Safari:**
1. Safari → Settings → Privacy
2. Uncheck: "Allow cookies and website data"
(App won't work)

**Note:** Disabling essential cookies breaks login functionality.

## 11. Do Not Track (DNT)

Your browser may send a "Do Not Track" signal. Currently, there is no industry-wide standard for responding to DNT.

We honor DNT for analytics:
- If DNT is enabled, we don't track usage data
- We still collect essential session data (required for app to work)

To enable DNT:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Sync and Google services → Click "Send Do Not Track request"
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy → Check "Send sites a 'Do not track' signal"
- **Safari:** Safari → Settings → Privacy → Check "Ask websites not to track me"
