## Special Cases: Amex Sept 18 Split

Certain Amex Platinum benefits use **claimingWindowEnd: "0918"** to indicate Sept 18 as the mid-year boundary (not standard Sept 1 or Oct 1).

### Affected Benefits

1. **Hotel Credit H1/H2** (SEMI_ANNUAL)
   - H1: Jan 1–Sept 17 = $200
   - H2: Sept 18–Dec 31 = $300 (50% MORE!)
   - Window End: "0918"

2. **Dining Credit** (QUARTERLY)
   - Q1 (Amex): Sept 18–Sept 30 (only 12 days! TIGHT!)
   - Q2 (Amex): Oct 1–Dec 31
   - Q3 (Amex): Jan 1–Mar 31
   - Q4 (Amex): Apr 1–Sept 17 (long window, 169 days)
   - Window End: "0918"

3. **Lululemon** (QUARTERLY)
   - Same split as Dining
   - Window End: "0918"

4. **Saks Fifth Avenue** (SEMI_ANNUAL)
   - H1: Jan 1–Sept 17
   - H2: Sept 18–Dec 31
   - Window End: "0918"

## Implementation Notes

### Data Storage

- All amounts stored in **cents** to avoid floating-point precision issues
- claimingCadence is a VARCHAR(50) allowing future expansion
- claimingWindowEnd is nullable, only populated for special cases
- Index on claimingCadence for efficient querying

### Usage in App

1. **Dashboard**: Show period-specific limits and countdown urgency
2. **Modal**: Enforce claiming limits based on cadence
3. **History**: Track claimed amounts per period
4. **Notifications**: Alert on expiring windows (monthly most urgent)
5. **Loss Tracking**: Highlight unclaimed benefits
