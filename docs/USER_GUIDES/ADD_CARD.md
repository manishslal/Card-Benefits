# Add and Manage Cards

Manage your credit card portfolio manually or by import.

## 📖 What You Can Do

- **Add cards** — Manually add one card at a time
- **Edit cards** — Update card details (issuer, name, annual fee)
- **Archive cards** — Keep card data but remove from active list
- **Delete cards** — Permanently remove cards (cannot undo)
- **Manage multiple cards** — Track spouse's, family member's, or business cards

## ➕ Add a Card Manually

### Method: Click and Fill

**Step 1: Open Add Card Form**

1. Log in to your dashboard
2. Look for **"Add Card"** button
   - Usually in top right corner: **"+ Add Card"**
   - Or in your card list: **"Add New Card"**
3. Click it

**Step 2: Fill Required Fields**

You'll see a form with these fields:

| Field | Required? | What to Enter | Example |
|-------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **Card Issuer** | ✅ Yes | Name of bank/company | Chase, American Express, Discover |
| **Card Name** | ✅ Yes | Specific card product | Sapphire Preferred, Gold Card |
| **Annual Fee** | ✅ Yes | Yearly fee in USD | 95, 150, 0 (for no fee) |
