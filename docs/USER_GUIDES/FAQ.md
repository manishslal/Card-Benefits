# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to common questions about Card Benefits Tracker.

## Getting Started

### Q: What's the difference between this app and my credit card issuer's app?

**A:** Credit card issuer apps track rewards you've earned. This app helps you make *decisions* about which cards to keep, by tracking benefits and calculating actual value.

**Your issuer's app:** "You've earned 5,000 points this month"  
**Our app:** "This card costs $95/year and provides $500 in benefits - should you keep it?"

### Q: Do I need to enter my credit card numbers?

**A:** No! Never enter credit card numbers. We only need:
- Card issuer (bank name): Chase, Amex, etc.
- Card name: Sapphire Preferred, Gold Card, etc.
- Annual fee: How much it costs per year

We never connect to your bank or access your account.

### Q: Is my data safe?

**A:** Yes. We use:
- Encrypted database (passwords encrypted with Argon2)
- HTTPS encryption for all communication
- Secure session authentication (JWT)
- No third-party data sharing

See [Privacy Policy](../../PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.
